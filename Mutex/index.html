About Us

MUTEX is the biggest scientific and technical annual event hosted by IEEE Zagazig Student Branch. The event mainly focuses on introducing the latest technologies in computer science fields to the audience and setting a clear path for them to follow.

Vision

Banishing the gap between the latest technology trends and the current community awareness.

Mission

Establishing a link between the leading companies/organizations in latest technologies and the academic students through sets of talks, trainings and workshops.
